The city of Townsville is a beautiful place, home of super heroines The Powerpuff Girls. The three powerpuff girls are Blossom, Bubbles, and Buttercup.
Fun Fact - Townsville is also the name of two other cities which actually exist in two different parts of the world: Townsville, North Carolina, United States and Townsville, Queensland, Australia.
